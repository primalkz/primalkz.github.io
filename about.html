ABOUT ME

Who am I?

I'm a developer! It all started with curiosity about how things work. I began by rooting my Redmi 1s in 8th grade, diving into Android custom ROMs, and that freedom to customize turned me into a developer. That curiosity grew into a love for open source software and the GNU/Linux ecosystem, which led me to squeeze every bit of performance out of my old potato PC. My first distro was Solus OS back in 2018.

I met many similar minded people on Telegram, both on Android and Linux groups. I also met bugswriter (Suraj Kushwah) in 2020 in a group called GNU/Linux India before he started his new YouTube channel, where we had discussions about games with his friend Shivam. Suraj inspired me to tinker and use Linux the hard way. I was already trying out different distros and beautifying desktop setups on Linux. I riced a few setups, posted tutorials on YouTube and shared on Reddit, trying out Gentoo, Arch, Void, then Nix. I connected with some amazing people shoutout to my tg friends cyberknight777, PeroSar(Ayush), mangesh, CyberSecByte and more.

I played around with editors like Vim and Emacs, learnt bash and system administration involuntarily, basically I picked it all up just by enjoying the process. Finally I settled down and came back with Arch, rocking it since then for gaming and everything. I use bspwm/dwm mainly on my desktop and Hyprland on my laptop.

I used to be paranoid about privacy after consooming lots of Luke Smith & Richard Stallman, but let's be real following their philosophy completely is basically impossible without becoming socially secluded and living in a cave. I tried to propagate RMS's ideas during my college days but the FOSS ideology wasn't strong in my batch, but I did make one good friend install Linux @amanhex haha.

You can find all my dotfiles at https://github.com/primalkz although the most recent one is Hyprland, but the others will still work. There's xmonad, suckless repo for dwm and their software, bspwm dotfiles etc, and emacs config inside the https://github.com/primalkz/dotfiles repo.
